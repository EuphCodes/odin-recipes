Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is one of my favorite go-to meals. It's so easy to customize and make to feed a lrage crowd.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups day old rice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup fresh, frozen or canned peas
  • 1/2 cup fresh, frozen or canned carrots
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen shelled edamame
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Add button to a large pan or wok and heat on medium to high heat
  2. Add onions and cook 2 minutes
  3. Add chopped veggies and cook 2 minutes
  4. Crack eggs into pan and scrambled
  5. Add cold rice and cook for another 2-3 minutes, until rice is heated through
  6. Mix soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, and sugar in small dish, add to heating rice and veggies
