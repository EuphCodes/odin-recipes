Halifax Donair

Description

Donairs have been one of my favorite meals for as long as I can remember. Now that I'm an adult I find making them at home to be the best way to enjoy them. While the process takes a bit of effort the taste when you can control the seasonings is much better than anything you can buy.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/4 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp flour
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pita

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 375
  2. Place ground beef in a large metal bowl
  3. Add all seasoning to ground beef
  4. Blend seasonings in with ground beef well. The seasonings should be mixed in well
  5. Knead the seasoned beef by forming all ball and throwing it into the bowl, repeat 20 times
  6. Shape well kneaded beef into a loaf
  7. Bake for 60-95 minutes, until no longer pink
  8. Slice tomato and onion while loaf is cooking
  9. Slice and serve on warmed pita with garlic donair sauce and chopped veggies
