Easy Air Fryer Salmon

Description

This salmon is made with simple ingredients and takes only 12 minutes from preperation to having a hot bite.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Tonys Cajun Creole Seasoning
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Place salmon skin side down on a plate or cutting board
  2. Apply olive oil to salmon top
  3. Sprinkle seasonings onto of salmon
  4. Place seasoned salmon skin side down in air fryer basket
  5. Cook salmon in air fryer at 380 for 10-12 minutes depending on your prefences
